<p><strong>The Two-Wheeler Electric Fuel Injector Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solenoid Type</li><li>Piezo Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The two-wheeler electric fuel injector market features two primary types: solenoid and piezo injectors. Solenoid injectors use electromagnetic coils to open and close the nozzle, providing precise fuel delivery and a simple design. They are widely used due to their reliability and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, piezo injectors employ piezoelectric crystals to achieve faster and more accurate fuel injection. This type allows for improved engine performance and efficiency, appealing to high-end applications and modern motorcycle designs.</p></p>
