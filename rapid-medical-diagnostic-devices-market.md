<p><p>The Rapid Medical Diagnostic Devices market encompasses various technologies, including lateral flow tests, agglutination assays, flow-through assays, and biosensors. Lateral flow tests are simple and quick, often used for home testing. Agglutination assays detect the aggregation of particles, offering sensitivity for specific diagnostics. Flow-through assays involve liquid samples flowing through a membrane for analysis. Biosensors combine biological components with electronic elements for real-time detection, providing rapid and accurate results for various medical conditions, enhancing patient care and diagnosis.</p></p>
