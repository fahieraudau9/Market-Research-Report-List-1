<p><p>Particle size analysis is essential for various industries, categorized into three main types: wet dispersion, dry dispersion, and spray dispersion. Wet dispersion involves suspending particles in a liquid, enhancing accuracy for fine particles. Dry dispersion utilizes air or mechanical forces to analyze dry powders, suitable for larger particles. Spray dispersion combines liquid and air to create an aerosol-like mist, allowing for a detailed analysis of fine particles. Each method serves distinct applications depending on the materials and desired outcomes.</p></p>
