<p><ul><li>One Component</li><li>Two Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive hemming adhesive market is categorized into one-component and two-component types. One-component adhesives are ready to use, requiring no mixing, and offer convenience and faster application processes. They typically cure through moisture or heat. In contrast, two-component adhesives involve mixing a resin and a hardener before application, providing superior bonding strength and resistance to various environmental factors. The choice between these types depends on specific application requirements, production speed, and desired performance characteristics in vehicle manufacturing.</p></p>
