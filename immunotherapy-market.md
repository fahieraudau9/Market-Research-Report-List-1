<p><p>The immunotherapy market consists of various types aimed at enhancing the immune response against diseases, particularly cancer. Monoclonal antibodies target specific antigens to inhibit tumor growth. Therapeutic vaccines stimulate the immune system to fight against cancer cells. Checkpoint inhibitors block proteins that suppress immune responses, allowing the immune system to attack tumors more effectively. Non-specific immunotherapy activates the immune system broadly, enhancing its ability to combat various diseases without targeting specific antigens. Each type plays a crucial role in advancing cancer treatment.</p></p>
