<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive Micromotor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Brushed DC Motor</li><li>Brushless DC Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive micromotor market includes two main types: brushed DC motors and brushless DC motors. Brushed DC motors utilize carbon brushes to transfer current, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for applications like window lift systems and actuators. Conversely, brushless DC motors eliminate brushes, resulting in higher efficiency, lower maintenance, and enhanced durability. They are commonly used in electric vehicle powertrains and advanced driver-assistance systems due to their superior performance and responsiveness, driving the demand in modern automotive applications.</p></p>
