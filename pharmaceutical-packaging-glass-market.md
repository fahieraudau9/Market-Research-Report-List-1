<p><p>The pharmaceutical packaging glass market is primarily categorized into two types: colored glass and colorless glass. Colored glass is utilized to protect light-sensitive medications from degradation, thereby enhancing product stability and shelf life. It often comes in various hues, such as amber or green, which block specific wavelengths of light. Colorless glass, on the other hand, offers a transparent view of the medication and is commonly used for products that are not sensitive to light. Both types are crucial for ensuring drug efficacy and safety.</p></p>
