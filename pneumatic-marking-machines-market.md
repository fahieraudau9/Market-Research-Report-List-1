<p><p>Pneumatic marking machines are classified into three main types: hand-held, bench top, and integrated. Hand-held machines are portable and ideal for marking large or irregular surfaces. Bench top models are stationary, designed for high-precision marks on smaller items, often used in production lines. Integrated machines combine marking systems with other manufacturing equipment for seamless operation, enhancing efficiency in automated environments. Each type caters to specific marking needs, balancing portability, precision, and integration in various industrial applications.</p></p>
