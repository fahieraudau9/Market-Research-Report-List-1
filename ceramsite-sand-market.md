<p><p>Ceramsite sand is categorized into three primary market types based on its shape: gravel-shaped, spherical, and cylindrical. Gravel-shaped ceramsite is characterized by its rough texture, providing excellent drainage and structural support, making it ideal for construction. Spherical ceramsite offers uniformity and better flow characteristics, often used in lightweight concrete and filtration systems. Cylindrical ceramsite, with its elongated form, is beneficial for applications requiring enhanced stability and packing efficiency, such as lightweight aggregate in various construction projects.</p></p>
