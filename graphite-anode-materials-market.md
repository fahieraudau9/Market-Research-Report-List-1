<p><p>The graphite anode materials market is primarily divided into two types: artificial graphite and natural graphite. Artificial graphite is synthesized from petroleum coke and offers superior performance in terms of energy density and charge/discharge efficiency, making it suitable for high-demand applications like electric vehicles and energy storage systems. In contrast, natural graphite is derived from mining and is more environmentally friendly but may have variations in quality and performance. Both types play crucial roles in the development of advanced battery technologies.</p></p>
