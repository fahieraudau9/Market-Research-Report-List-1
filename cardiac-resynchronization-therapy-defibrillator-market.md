<p><p>Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy Defibrillator (CRT-D) is an advanced therapeutic device designed for patients with heart failure, particularly those with ventricular dysynchrony. It combines the functions of a pacemaker and an implantable cardioverter-defibrillator, delivering synchronized electrical stimulation to enhance the heart's pumping efficiency while also defibrillating in case of life-threatening arrhythmias. This dual functionality makes CRT-D devices pivotal in managing chronic heart failure and improving patient outcomes.</p><p>The cardiac resynchronization therapy defibrillator market is poised for significant growth, driven by an aging population, increasing prevalence of heart failure, and advancements in medical technology.
