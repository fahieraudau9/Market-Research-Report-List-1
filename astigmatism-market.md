<p><strong>The Astigmatism Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Myopic Astigmatism</li><li>Hyperopic Astigmatism</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The astigmatism market can be segmented into two primary types: myopic astigmatism and hyperopic astigmatism. Myopic astigmatism occurs when the cornea or lens is unevenly curved, leading to blurred vision for distant objects, typically affecting individuals who are nearsighted. Hyperopic astigmatism, on the other hand, occurs when the curvature leads to difficulty focusing on close objects, often seen in farsighted individuals. Both types require corrective measures, such as glasses or contact lenses, to improve vision clarity and quality of life.</p></p>
