<p><ul><li>Hydrophobic Coating Technology</li><li>Nanofilm Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive hydrophobic glass market consists of two main types: hydrophobic coating technology and nanofilm technology. Hydrophobic coating technology involves applying a chemical treatment that repels water, enhancing visibility and reducing the accumulation of dirt and grime on windshields and windows. Conversely, nanofilm technology uses ultra-thin, transparent films that provide similar water-repelling properties while also offering durability and protection against scratches and environmental damage. Both technologies aim to improve safety, maintenance, and overall driving experience by enhancing visibility during adverse weather conditions.</p></p>
