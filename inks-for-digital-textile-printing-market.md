<p><strong>The Inks for Digital Textile Printing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reactive Dye Inks</li><li>Acidic Ink</li><li>Paint Ink</li><li>Dispersion & Sublimation Ink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the digital textile printing market, various ink types cater to different fabric and design needs. Reactive dye inks bond chemically with cellulose fibers, providing vibrant colors and durability. Acidic inks are ideal for protein fibers like silk and wool, offering excellent color brilliance. Paint inks, versatile and easy to use, provide a wide range of finishes. Lastly, dispersion and sublimation inks are primarily used for synthetic fabrics, ensuring vivid prints that penetrate the material for lasting effects.</p></p>
