<p><p>Post-column flow splitters are devices used in liquid chromatography to divide the flow of a mobile phase after it exits the column, enabling simultaneous detection and analysis. The market types include:</p><p>1. **Double Fixed Splitters**: These offer a constant, predefined split ratio, ensuring consistent analysis but limited flexibility.</p><p>2. **Replaceable Fixed Splitters**: These allow users to switch between different fixed split ratios by changing the splitter, providing some adaptability.</p><p>3. **Adjustable Splitters**: These enable real-time modification of split ratios, offering the highest level of customization for varied analytical needs.</p></p>
