<p><p>Mechanically Fastened Cross-laminated Timber (CLT) is an engineered wood product created by layering lumber boards in perpendicular orientations and bonding them with mechanical fasteners instead of adhesives. This innovative approach enhances structural performance, durability, and resistance to environmental factors. </p><p>The market for Mechanically Fastened CLT is witnessing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for sustainable and eco-friendly building materials, coupled with rising awareness of the carbon footprint associated with traditional construction methods.
