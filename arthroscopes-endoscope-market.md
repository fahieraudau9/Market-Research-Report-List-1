<p><p>The arthroscopes endoscope market is categorized into three main types: straight, semi-flexible, and bent arthroscopes. Straight arthroscopes offer a rigid design for direct access, ideal for straightforward joint examinations. Semi-flexible arthroscopes combine rigidity with some flexibility, allowing movement in tight spaces while retaining image clarity. Bent arthroscopes are highly flexible, enabling navigation through complex anatomical structures, making them suitable for intricate procedures. Each type serves specific clinical needs, facilitating minimally invasive surgeries and improving diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>
