<p><p>The Compact Machine Room Elevator market encompasses two primary types: traction and hydraulic elevators. Traction elevators utilize ropes and pulleys, providing energy efficiency and reduced space requirements, making them ideal for medium to high-rise buildings. In contrast, hydraulic elevators use fluid-driven pistons, suitable for low-rise applications and offering easier installation and maintenance. Both types are designed to optimize space and improve functionality, contributing to the growing demand for compact solutions in urban environments and modern structures.</p></p>
