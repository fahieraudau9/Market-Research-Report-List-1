<p><strong>The Automotive Logic ICs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SMD Mounting Style</li><li>SMT Mounting Style</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive logic ICs market is primarily categorized into two mounting styles: SMD (Surface-Mount Device) and SMT (Surface-Mount Technology). SMD refers to components designed for surface mounting, allowing for compact designs and efficient use of circuit board space. SMT encompasses the entire assembly process where these components are installed on the surface of PCBs, facilitating automated production. Both styles enhance reliability and performance in automotive applications, supporting advanced electronic systems in modern vehicles.</p></p>
