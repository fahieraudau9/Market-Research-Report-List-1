<p><p>The Media Gateway market is categorized into Analog and Digital segments, each serving different communication needs. Analog media gateways facilitate the conversion of traditional analog signals, such as those from PSTN lines, into digital formats suitable for IP networks. In contrast, Digital media gateways handle purely digital signals, enabling seamless connectivity between various digital communication systems, including VoIP and IP-based services. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring interoperability between legacy systems and modern communication networks.</p></p>
