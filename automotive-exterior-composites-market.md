<p><p>The automotive exterior composites market is segmented into various types based on manufacturing processes. Compression-molded composites involve pressing heated materials into molds for strong, durable parts. Injection-molded composites use melted materials injected into molds for precise shapes and quick production. Resin-infused composites involve soaking reinforcement materials with resin under vacuum, producing lightweight, rigid components. Additionally, the "Others" category encompasses emerging technologies and hybrid processes, broadening the applications for exterior automotive components while enhancing performance and aesthetics.</p></p>
