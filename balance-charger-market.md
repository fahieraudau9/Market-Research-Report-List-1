<p><p>The balance charger market includes two primary types: AC and DC balance chargers. AC balance chargers are designed for household or commercial use, plugging into standard electrical outlets to charge multiple lithium batteries simultaneously while ensuring balanced charging. In contrast, DC balance chargers are often used in automotive or portable applications, requiring a direct current source, such as car batteries or solar panels. Both types focus on optimizing battery performance and longevity by maintaining equal voltage levels across individual cells.</p></p>
