<p><p>Marketing dashboards can be categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based dashboards are hosted on remote servers, allowing users easy access from any device with internet connectivity. This flexibility supports real-time data updates and collaboration. In contrast, on-premise dashboards are installed locally on a company's servers, providing enhanced control over data security and customization. Both types serve the purpose of visualizing marketing performance metrics, enabling informed decision-making and strategic planning.</p></p>
