<p><strong>The Corrugated Carton Ink Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-Based Inks</li><li>Solvent-Based Inks</li><li>UV-Cured Inks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The corrugated carton ink market features three main types: water-based inks, solvent-based inks, and UV-cured inks. Water-based inks are eco-friendly and suitable for high-speed printing, offering good adhesion and vibrant colors. Solvent-based inks provide excellent durability and resistance but may emit volatile organic compounds. UV-cured inks utilize ultraviolet light to instantly cure the ink, resulting in high gloss and strong adhesion, ideal for intricate designs. Each type serves distinct application needs in the packaging industry, optimizing performance and environmental impact.</p></p>
