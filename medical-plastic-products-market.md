<p><p>The medical plastic products market consists of various polymer types, each utilized for specific applications. PVC (polyvinyl chloride) is commonly used for medical bags and tubing due to its flexibility and chemical resistance. PP (polypropylene) offers sterility and high-temperature tolerance, making it ideal for surgical instruments and containers. PE (polyethylene) is favored for packaging and disposable items due to its durability. PS (polystyrene) serves in applications like trays and test tubes, while "Others" includes specialized polymers for niche medical uses.</p></p>
