<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Supply Equipment (EVSE) industry comprises three market types: Level 1, Level 2, and Level 3 charging stations. Level 1 provides standard household outlets, delivering low charging power suited for overnight charging at home. Level 2 stations offer faster charging using 240V circuits, making them ideal for home and public use. Level 3, or DC fast chargers, provide rapid charging for commercial applications and highway charging, significantly reducing wait times and enhancing electric vehicle usability on long trips.</p></p>
