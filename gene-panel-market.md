<p><strong>The Gene Panel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Amplicon-based Approach</li><li>Hybridization-based Approach</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gene panel market features two primary approaches: amplicon-based and hybridization-based. The amplicon-based approach utilizes PCR amplification to selectively target and enrich specific genomic regions of interest, allowing for high sensitivity and accuracy in variant detection. In contrast, the hybridization-based approach relies on specific probes that bind to target sequences, facilitating the capture and sequencing of multiple genes simultaneously. Both methods cater to the increasing demand for personalized medicine, cancer genomics, and heritable disease assessment.</p></p>
