<p><p>The rail traction converter market comprises two main types: Propulsion Converters and Integrated Compact Converters. Propulsion Converters are specialized systems that convert electrical energy into mechanical power, driving the train's wheels. In contrast, Integrated Compact Converters combine multiple functions, including traction and auxiliary power supply, into a single, space-efficient unit. Both types are essential for enhancing energy efficiency and performance in modern rail systems, contributing to greater reliability and reduced maintenance costs in the railway sector.</p></p>
