<p><p>The plain bearings market for motors includes various types designed for specific applications. Bushings provide low-friction surfaces for rotating parts. Journal bearings support rotating shafts, offering stability and load distribution. Sleeve bearings are cylindrical and facilitate smooth movement between components. Riffle bearings feature grooves to enhance lubrication and reduce wear. Composite bearings combine different materials to improve performance, durability, and wear resistance. Each type serves unique functions in reducing friction and supporting motor efficiency in diverse industries.</p></p>
