<p><ul><li>Light Liquid Paraffin</li><li>Heavy Liquid Paraffin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid paraffin market is segmented into two main types: light liquid paraffin and heavy liquid paraffin. Light liquid paraffin, known for its low viscosity and transparency, is commonly used in cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and food applications due to its emollient properties. Heavy liquid paraffin, on the other hand, is thicker and more viscous, making it suitable for industrial applications, lubricants, and as a base for various chemical processes. Both types are valued for their stable and inert characteristics.</p></p>
