<p><p>The belt filter market comprises two primary types: horizontal and vertical belt filters. Horizontal belt filters utilize a flat horizontal surface for the filtration process, making them suitable for high-capacity operations and applications with larger particles. In contrast, vertical belt filters operate on a vertical plane, optimizing space and typically handling slurries with varying flow rates. Both types are critical in industries like mining, chemicals, and wastewater treatment, where efficient solid-liquid separation is essential.</p></p>
