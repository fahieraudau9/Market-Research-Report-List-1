<p><strong>The Railway Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gate Valves</li><li>Control Valves</li><li>Ball Valves</li><li>Butterfly Valve</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The railway valves market consists of various types essential for controlling fluid flow in rail systems. Gate valves are primarily used for on/off flow control, providing minimal resistance. Control valves regulate flow and pressure, allowing precise management of systems. Ball valves offer reliable sealing with a quick operation, suitable for stop/start functions. Butterfly valves are lightweight and efficient for throttling applications. Other valves may include check valves, pressure relief valves, and safety valves, each serving specific roles in railway operations.</p></p>
