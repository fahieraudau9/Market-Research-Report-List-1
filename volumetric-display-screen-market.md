<p><ul><li>Static Volume</li><li>Swept Volume</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The volumetric display screen market can be categorized into two main types: static volume and swept volume displays. Static volume displays create three-dimensional images that remain fixed in a specific location, allowing for detailed examination of 3D content. In contrast, swept volume displays generate dynamic images by rotating or projecting light across a defined space, enabling real-time interaction and movement. Both types serve various applications, including medical imaging, entertainment, and simulations, enhancing visualization and user engagement.</p></p>
