<p><p>The disposable hemoperfusion market is primarily categorized into two types: charcoal hemoperfusion and certain resins hemoperfusion. Charcoal hemoperfusion utilizes activated charcoal to adsorb a wide range of toxins from the bloodstream, effectively enhancing detoxification. Conversely, certain resins hemoperfusion employs specialized resin materials tailored for selectively removing specific harmful substances, such as bile acids or cytokines. Both methods aim to improve patient outcomes by effectively purifying the blood during critical medical conditions, thereby addressing diverse therapeutic needs.</p></p>
